What is the STEM strand?

The STEM strand offers a wide range of career opportunities for graduates. With the increasing demand for professionals in STEM fields, students who graduate from this program have excellent prospects for finding well-paying jobs. Some of the career opportunities that are available for STEM graduates include:

  • Engineer - Engineers design and develop new products, processes, and systems. There are many different types of engineers, including mechanical engineers, electrical engineers, civil engineers, and aerospace engineers.
  • Scientist - Scientists conduct research to discover new knowledge about the world around us. There are many different types of scientists, including biologists, chemists, and physicists.
  • Data Analyst - Data analysts analyze and interpret large sets of data to identify patterns and trends. They work in a variety of fields, including marketing, finance, and healthcare.
  • Architect - Architects design buildings and structures. They work in a variety of settings, including residential, commercial, and industrial.
  • Medical professional - Medical professionals work in a variety of fields, including healthcare, biotechnology, and pharmaceuticals. They include doctors, nurses, and researchers.

Overall, the STEM strand offers students an excellent opportunity to pursue a career in fields that are in high demand. Graduates of this program have a wide range of career opportunities available to them, and they are well-equipped to meet the challenges of the 21st century.
